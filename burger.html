Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles

Instructions:

  1. Form ground beef into patties.
  2. Grill the patties until cooked to desired doneness.
  3. Toast the burger buns.
  4. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, cheese, ketchup, mustard, and pickles.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
